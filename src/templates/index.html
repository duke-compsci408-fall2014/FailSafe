Welcome to FailSafe

Failsafe is a web application that allows healthcare professionals to respond to emergencies in a timely manner. Not only does it allow users to check when they are scheduled to be on-call, but it also allows them to also let the operator know when they will be arriving at the hospital via an SMS messaging system. FailSafe allows the operator to additionally cycle through all kinds of contact information so that the activation system does not stop contacting each member of the on-call team until he or she responds through the messaging system.

The application was incepted by Drs. Schuyler Jones and Manesh Patel from Duke University Hospital, and the project was created by undergraduate members of the computer science department:
